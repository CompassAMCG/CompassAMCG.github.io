Embedding a Video into Your Website

This tutorial will guide you through the steps to embed a video using HTML.

Step 1: Prepare Your Video File

Make sure your video is in a web-friendly format such as MP4, WebM, or Ogg. For this tutorial, we will use an MP4 video hosted online.

Step 2: Add the <video> Tag

Use the <video> tag to embed the video into your HTML page. Include the controls attribute to provide playback controls to the user.

Step 3: Save and View Your Page

Save your HTML file and open it in a web browser to see the embedded video in action.
