Farewell to Citrus Cafe: A Tustin Favorite Closes

Local Restaurant to be Replaced by a Car Wash

After decades of serving the community, the beloved Citrus Cafe in Tustin, California, has closed its doors for the last time. Known for its welcoming atmosphere and delicious cuisine, the cafe was a staple for locals who cherished its cozy setting and friendly service.

Despite community support, rising costs and redevelopment pressures led to the decision to close. The space will soon be transformed into a car wash, a change that has sparked mixed reactions among residents.
